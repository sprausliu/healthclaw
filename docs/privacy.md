# Privacy Policy

**HealthClaw** is a self-hosted health data sync app for iOS.

## Data Collection

HealthClaw does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

- All health data synced by the app is sent **directly to your own self-hosted server**
- No data is transmitted to any third-party servers or services
- No analytics, tracking, or crash reporting
- The app does not require an account or login of any kind

## Health Data

HealthClaw reads health data from Apple HealthKit (heart rate, sleep, steps, workouts, and other metrics you authorize) and sends it exclusively to the webhook server URL you configure during setup. You own and control this server.
